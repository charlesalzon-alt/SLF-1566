SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This SMS Privacy Policy explains how PriceHubble handles information in connection with its text messaging (SMS) programs. It supplements, and should be read together with, the PriceHubble Privacy Policy.

No sharing of mobile information.

No mobile opt-in information or consent will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information collected for SMS is used only to operate the messaging programs described below.

Our messaging programs

  • Account and lead notifications (professionals): alerts to PriceHubble platform users about their own account and lead activity, such as new property requests, valuation updates, and client interactions.
  • Client follow-up (consumers): follow-up messages from a loan officer and their assistant to existing clients about their current loan, refinancing, and new financing options, and to schedule meetings.

Information we collect

  • Your mobile phone number.
  • The content of messages you exchange with us.
  • Delivery and engagement metadata (for example timestamps and opt-in or opt-out status).

How we use it

  • To send the messages you have opted in to receive.
  • To answer your questions and schedule meetings.
  • To honor opt-out requests and keep records required for our legal obligations.

How we share it

We share data only with service providers that help us operate the messaging programs (for example our SMS carrier and platform vendor), under contract and only as needed to deliver the service. As stated above, mobile opt-in information and consent are never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Message frequency and rates

Message frequency varies by program and by your activity. Message and data rates may apply.

Your choices

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP. You may also contact us using the details below.

Data retention

We retain message records for as long as needed to provide the service and to meet our business and legal obligations, then delete or anonymize them.

Contact

Questions about this SMS Privacy Policy: privacy@pricehubble.com.
