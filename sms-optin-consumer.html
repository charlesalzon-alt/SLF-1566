SMS Opt-in: Client Follow-up

Audience: existing clients of a PriceHubble loan officer (consumers) · Last updated: June 24, 2026

This page documents how end users consent to receive SMS from the client follow-up program, for A2P 10DLC campaign review. The program uses a double opt-in: the first message asks the recipient to confirm before any further messages are sent.

Who receives messages

Recipients are people who are already clients of the loan officer and who provided their mobile number during the mortgage origination process, as part of that existing relationship. The loan officer and their assistant follow up about the client's current loan, refinancing and new financing options, and to schedule a meeting.

How users opt in (call to action)

  1. The first SMS is a service follow-up that names the sender and asks the client to confirm by replying YES. Double opt-in
  2. No further messages are sent unless the client replies YES (active, affirmative consent).
  3. Every message identifies the sender and includes opt-out instructions.
  4. The client can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help.

Opt-in conversation

Messages
Hi Jane, this is Alex's assistant at PriceHubble. We worked together on your mortgage. Rates have moved since we closed. Can I send you a quick refinance check? Reply YES to continue, or STOP to opt out.

The conversation only continues after the client actively replies YES.

Required disclosures

  • Program: PriceHubble loan officer client follow-up.
  • Message frequency: recurring, up to 4 messages per month. Frequency may vary.
  • Cost: message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt-out: reply STOP. Help: reply HELP.
  • Privacy: SMS Privacy Policy. No mobile opt-in information or consent is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Terms: SMS Terms and Conditions.
