SMS Opt-in: Account and Lead Notifications

Audience: PriceHubble platform users (real estate and mortgage professionals) · Last updated: June 24, 2026

This page documents how end users consent to receive SMS from the PriceHubble account and lead notification program, for A2P 10DLC campaign review.

Who receives messages

Recipients are existing PriceHubble customers who have a PriceHubble account. Messages are transactional notifications about the user's own account and lead activity (new property requests, valuation updates, client interactions).

How users opt in (call to action)

  1. The user signs in to their account at app.pricehubble.com.
  2. They open Settings > Notifications.
  3. They enter their mobile number and actively check the SMS consent box. Unchecked by default
  4. They save. SMS consent is optional and is not a condition of using PriceHubble.

Consent language (exact)

"I agree to receive SMS notifications from PriceHubble about my account and lead activity (new property requests, valuation updates, client interactions). Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help."

Required disclosures

  • Program: PriceHubble account and lead notifications.
  • Message frequency: recurring, varies with account activity.
  • Cost: message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt-out: reply STOP. Help: reply HELP.
  • Privacy: SMS Privacy Policy. No mobile opt-in information or consent is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Terms: SMS Terms and Conditions.

Sample messages

  • "PriceHubble: one of your leads requested a callback. View the details in your dashboard: https://app.pricehubble.com . Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help."
  • "PriceHubble: a new valuation request was submitted by one of your prospects. Review it in your dashboard: https://app.pricehubble.com . Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help."
